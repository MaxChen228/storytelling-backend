</Person1><Person1>Mmm, that phrase, “stake everything,” is a great one. It means to risk all of your resources on a single chance, like a gambler pushing all their chips into the center of the table. Sutt is essentially saying the Empire won’t bet its future on a small conflict like this.
</Person1><Person1>But Mallow… Mallow sees the bigger picture.
