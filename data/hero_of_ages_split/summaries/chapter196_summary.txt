Zane tempts Vin as she grows close to the kandra OreSeur. Sazed returns with news of mists causing sickness and a koloss army marching on Luthadel. Elend, though deposed, begins to understand leadership.

The koloss attack, leading to a siege and many losses. Vin, deceived and sent away, returns to find Luthadel. She discovers OreSeur is TenSoon, kills Zane, then controls the koloss, saving the city and crowning Elend emperor. Vin finds the Well of Ascension beneath Luthadel.

Sazed, discovering prophecy errors, tries to stop Vin. But Vin, following what she believes is prophecy, releases the Well's power, inadvertently freeing a dark entity. Though Elend is saved by becoming Mistborn, the world now faces a new threat.
