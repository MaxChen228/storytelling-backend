Ancient lore of "The Balance" details physiological differences between skaa and nobility, now mostly erased by interbreeding. Elend's forces reach Fadrex City, Cett's new, naturally fortified capital. Defenders have cleared trees and dug trenches at its narrow canyon entrance, ready for siege.

Ham notes the formidable defenses and the canal clogging with ash, making return by boat impossible. He feels fate strands them, but Elend, pragmatic, orders camp setup and battle preparations. Ham's gaze lingers on the impassable canal, underscoring their irreversible commitment.
