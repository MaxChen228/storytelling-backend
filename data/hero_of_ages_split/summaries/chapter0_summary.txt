Marsh, an Inquisitor, struggles to die, but a powerful entity seizes control. It grants him new vision through his eye-spikes, revealing a terrified prisoner.

Marsh briefly resists, horrified. Yet, "It"—Ruin, freed by Vin from the Well of Ascension—is too strong. Ruin needs Marsh to find "something hidden," limited by an opposing force.

Marsh raises the spike, beginning the gruesome Hemalurgy. Listeners will soon discover what Ruin seeks and the true nature of its power.
