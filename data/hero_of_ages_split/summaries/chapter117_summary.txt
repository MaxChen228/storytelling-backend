Rashek, his power waning, balances his world-altering changes, creating a world of falling ash and brown plants. He names himself the "Sliver of Infinity."

Under Ruin's control, Marsh experiences brief lucidity, despairing he cannot fight or end his own life. He and other Inquisitors now bear new spikes, granting withheld abilities. Marsh finds these new powers beautiful.

What will these newly empowered Inquisitors do next?
