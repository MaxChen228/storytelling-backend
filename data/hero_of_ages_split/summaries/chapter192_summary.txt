The immortal Lord Ruler's Final Empire endures for a millennium until Kelsier, a legendary Mistborn, sparks a skaa rebellion. Kelsier, discovering Malatium's power to glimpse the past, challenges the Lord Ruler but is killed, becoming a martyr. His protégé, Vin, continues, infiltrating the palace to defeat the Lord Ruler, who issues a cryptic warning.

Vin then follows the Hero of Ages prophecy to the Well of Ascension, taking and relinquishing a great power, believing she saves the world. Yet, the prophecies were a ruse, designed to free the malevolent force, Ruin. During this time, the omnipresent Mists turn deadly, claiming lives.

Elend Venture rises as king, his army includes Kelsier's crew, facing threats from Koloss and terrifying Steel Inquisitors, Marsh now among their spiked ranks. Immortal kandra like TenSoon navigate contracts amidst chaos; Sazed, the last Terris Keeper, guards ancient knowledge. The unleashed power of Ruin and the deadly Mists now threaten the world.
