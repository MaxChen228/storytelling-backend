Elend debates King Yomen on the Lord Ruler's oppressive rule, struggling to hold the crowd's attention. When a dazed, bloodied woman is brought in, signaling Vin's distraction, Elend seizes the chance to test Yomen's suspected Allomancy. He attacks, Pushing onto the stage with glass daggers. Yomen initially seems unenhanced, but after Elend chokes him, Yomen retaliates with a hidden knife, wounding Elend. Burning electrum, Elend sees Yomen hesitate, confirming he is a Mistborn burning atium. Wounded, Elend escapes.

Back at camp, Elend anxiously awaits Vin, but she never returns. Despite his injuries, he prepares to search for her. A messenger arrives with a letter from Yomen. The king reveals he has captured Vin and admits he deliberately prolonged their debate to distract Elend, exploiting his overconfidence. Elend now faces a desperate race against time to rescue Vin from Yomen's clutches.
