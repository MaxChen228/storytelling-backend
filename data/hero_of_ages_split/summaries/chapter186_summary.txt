Ruin decapitates Elend, gloating as Vin watches. She feels a surprising peace, reflecting on their journey and the many losses. Refusing to let their sacrifices be in vain, Vin gathers her power.

She drives against Ruin, realizing Preservation's plan was to create beings capable of both protecting and destroying. Declaring Elend's death Ruin's fatal mistake, Vin, now embodying Preservation, pulls Ruin into an abyss with her, sacrificing herself to end its reign. The true impact of this ultimate sacrifice remains to be seen.
