Vin scouts Yomen's guarded palace, the Lord Ruler's cache. She plans to consult Elend before infiltrating. Vin senses a Mistborn following, burning copper and steel. Uniquely, she pierces their coppercloud, an ability shared with the Lord Ruler.

The unaware pursuer keeps pace until Vin PUSHES off the city gates, making the Allomancer's pulses vanish. Confused by the sudden disappearance, Vin considers the enigmatic mist spirit, unseen for a year.

Vin returns to Elend's camp, pondering the mystery. She must now uncover the identity of her elusive follower.
