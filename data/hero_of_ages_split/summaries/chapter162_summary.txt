Exhausted, TenSoon sees the mists flow purposefully towards Luthadel. Hope renewed, he gallops back. In Luthadel, Breeze and Allrianne notice the mists accelerating, while an injured Spook bursts in, urgently commanding them to gather everyone for the storage cavern.

Meanwhile, Elend and Ham fight a losing battle against koloss outside Fadrex. Elend, noticing the mists flowing purposefully, soon learns the koloss are suddenly retreating at inhuman speed, confirming their rapid movement towards Luthadel.

The converging paths of mists, koloss, and the urgent call to shelter suggest a monumental event is rapidly approaching.
