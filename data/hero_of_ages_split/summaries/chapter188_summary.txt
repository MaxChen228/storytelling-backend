Sazed wields the combined, opposing powers of Ruin and Preservation, his unified will allowing them to create. Drawing on all Keeper knowledge, he uncovers ancient truths within mankind's religions. He meticulously restores the world: reshaping continents, repositioning the planet, reviving human physiology, animal life, and vibrant colors in plants and sky, guided by these diverse faiths.

He realizes these religions, though not wholly true, each contained vital truths. Completing his work, Sazed restores beauty and life, yet the immense power remains with him, endless. He understands he is the Hero of Ages, destined to span all ages and preserve mankind as a new, unified deity. He ponders how these powers were originally split, a mystery that lingers.
