Wayne confesses his past, revealing he killed a man and now sends money to the victim's family, who still resent him. His profound guilt prevents him from holding a gun steadily.

Waxillium arrives, reporting that the Vanishers are moving large machinery via canal boats at night. Wayne presents a cigar box he found, which Marasi notes is expensive and contains mysterious numbers. Waxillium recognizes the significance, asking if Marasi knows Miles Dagouter, a lawkeeper. He then announces they must take a trip.
