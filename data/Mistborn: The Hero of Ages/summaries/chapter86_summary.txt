Elend asserts control, commanding koloss to aid wounded and demanding Fatren's deference. Eager, Vin joins them. She worries about Elend fighting alone, but he explains strategic necessity, highlighting their dynamic of love and trust.

At the gates, Elend's silent command holds thousands of koloss at bay, impressing fearful soldiers. Inside, Vin uses her koloss servant to carry the dead Inquisitor, accustoming citizens to them. They proceed to the ransacked Ministry building, dismissed by Fatren as empty. Elend reveals the Ministry's true purpose: researching Allomantic metals and Terris bloodlines, hinting at a crucial secret.

Vin quickly uncovers a hidden trapdoor to a wine cellar, then finds a secret wall. Elend and Vin combine Allomantic powers, burning steel and iron with pewter's strength, to force open a massive, concealed door. This intricate mechanism, impenetrable without Allomancy, hints at its importance. What ancient secrets await them inside?
