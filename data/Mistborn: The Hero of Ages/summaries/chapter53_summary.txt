Vin uses emotional Allomancy, intensifying the women's fear as she confronts Lady Patresen. She asserts her power, reminding Patresen of a noble house Vin destroyed, and warns that her overwhelming forces, including koloss and Mistborn, will take the city. Vin contrasts her husband's honor with her own lack of temperance, delivering a powerful Soothing to emphasize her dominance.

Her true goal is to be seen challenging Patresen to define her political position. Unexpectedly, Patresen's clique abandons their leader, approaching Vin to introduce her at the party. Surprised, Vin agrees. She is now drawn into the social dynamics of the party.
