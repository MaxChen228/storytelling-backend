Marsh, controlled by Ruin, tortures Vin, but his mind rebels. Haunted by failure, he refuses to give up again. Recognizing Vin's earring as a spike, recalling Spook's warning, Marsh seizes Ruin's weakened control and rips it from her ear.

Vin awakens, Ruin's voice silenced. In agony, she reaches for mists, remembering their power from the Well of Ascension. As Marsh raises his axe, Vin draws in the mists. Her pain vanishes; surging with strength, she catches his arm, poised for confrontation.
