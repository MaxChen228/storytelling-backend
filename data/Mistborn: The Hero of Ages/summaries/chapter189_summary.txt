Preservation, a weakened mist spirit, chooses Vin from a young age, grooming her to inherit his power. Her exceptional Allomancy and early Snapping make her unique; she draws the mists, even using a fragment of Preservation's power to defeat the Lord Ruler before touching the Well of Ascension.

The narrator now ponders this profound mystery, recognizing Vin's worthiness above all others who touched the power. They hint at their own expanded mind and ability to wield such forces. Listeners will soon discover the full extent of this new understanding.
